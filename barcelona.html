1. Sagrada de Familia

The Basílica de la Sagrada Família, also known as the Sagrada Família, is a large unfinished Roman Catholic minor basilica in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain.

Hours:

November through February: 9 am to 6 pm.
March and October: 9 am to 7 pm.
April through September: 9 am to 8 pm.
25 and 26 December, 1 and 6 January: 9 am to 2 pm.

Tickets:

Adult (30-64): 20,00 €
Under 30: 18,00 €
Children: Free
Audioguide in English: 4,50 €
